About Me

Growth marketing built on data, creativity & a personal touch.

I'm an accomplished performance and growth marketing manager with over 10 years of paid and inbound experience. Throughout my career I've honed the craft of building data-driven strategies that optimize performance and fuel growth for SaaS and B2C companies — managing multi-million-dollar budgets and the cross-functional teams that bring campaigns to life.

I thrive in fast-paced, agile environments where creativity and experimentation are encouraged. My background spanning business, psychology, and marketing means campaigns get executed with precision and messaging that actually resonates. I'm just as comfortable distilling complex data into clear recommendations for C-suite executives as I am in the weeds of a Meta or Google account.

Outside of work, I'm a husband and a dad to one daughter, based in Denver, Colorado. Family keeps me grounded — and is a big part of why I care so much about doing work I'm proud of.
